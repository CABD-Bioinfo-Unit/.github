# CABD Bioinformatics Unit 🧬

Welcome to the **CABD Bioinformatics Unit** — the bioinformatics support and research group at the [Centro Andaluz de Biología del Desarrollo (CABD)](https://www.cabd.es/), a joint research center of the Spanish National Research Council (CSIC), the University Pablo de Olavide, and the Junta de Andalucía, located in Seville, Spain.

## What We Do

We provide bioinformatics expertise and computational support to researchers at CABD and collaborate on a wide range of projects in developmental and evolutionary biology, including:

- 🔬 Genomics & transcriptomics analysis (RNA-seq, ChIP-seq, ATAC-seq, …)
- 🧪 Single-cell sequencing data analysis
- 🐟 Comparative genomics and genome assembly
- 📊 Statistical analysis and data visualization
- 💻 Pipeline development and workflow automation
